How DRIVEGUARD Works

This system monitors the driver's facial landmarks in real-time to detect drowsiness. It tracks eye closure and head movements to determine the driver's alertness level. When drowsiness is detected, an alarm is triggered to alert the driver.

Drowsiness detection is important to prevent accidents caused by driver fatigue.

The system uses the following steps:

  1. Facial landmark detection.
  2. Eye closure monitoring.
  3. Head movement tracking.
  4. Drowsiness detection and alarm triggering.
